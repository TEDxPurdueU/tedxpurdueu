November Salon Event!

November 9, 5:00 PM - 7:30 PM @ Class of 1950 Lecture Hall

Our second Salon event of the year is fast approaching! This event will feature talks on our lives in the future, and living for that future, for sustainability. We'll share more details as we get closer!

About TEDx

TEDxPurdueU is about showcasing the ingenuity of the Purdue University and West Lafayette community by pulling together the best speakers, students, and community members we have for a day of ideas and innovation.
