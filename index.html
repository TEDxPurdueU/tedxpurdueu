November Event: Back to the Future: Tomorrow's Technology Today

November 9, 6:30 PM - 7:30 PM @ Class of 1950 Lecture Hall

Thanks to everyone who came for a wonderful evening! Our second Salon event of the 2016-17 season featured talks on our lives in the future, and living for that future, for sustainability. Everything from breakthroughs in farming technologies to Minority Report-style experiences that we'll find in our daily lives in just a few decades, we saw glimpses of our future in the world around us.

About TEDx

TEDxPurdueU is about showcasing the ingenuity of the Purdue University and West Lafayette community by pulling together the best speakers, students, and community members we have for a day of ideas and innovation.
