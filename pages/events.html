TEDxPurdueUSalon: Back to the Future

November 9, 6:30 PM - 7:30 PM @ Class of 1950 Lecture Hall

Our second Salon event of the 2016-17 season is fast approaching! This event will feature talks on our lives in the future, and living for that future, for sustainability. Join us to talk about everything from breakthroughs in farming technologies to Minority Report-style experiences that we'll find in our daily lives in just a few decades. We'll share more details as we get closer!

TEDxPurdueU 2016: Tear Up the Box

April 9, 2:00 PM - 9:00 PM

TEDxPurdueU, an independently organized TED (Technology, Entertainment, Design) event, uses storytelling to nurture a culture of innovation through sharing ideas developed by Purdue students, faculty, alumni, and community members with the global TED community. At TEDxPurdueU, several live speakers from diverse fields and schools of thought, such as history, science, education, the arts, engineering, business, and technology, will combine to spark deep discussion and interconnection among our community of lifelong learners.
